SURFACTANT EOR

The production, separation and purification of crude oil and gas need to be achieved not only safely but also quickly, economically, and in compliance with the regulatory restrictions of the operator's environment.
Major strides have been made in the scale and speed of production, fueled by advances in understanding the characteristics of produced fluids, improvements in engineering design and materials science, and a greater appreciation of the mechanisms and conditions that cause production problems. However, the task remains complex, and the need to develop specialty chemical products to help meet productivity targets continues to grow.

Panjilaborta Chemindo provides an extensive range of products that as formulated additives, can address most of the compelling issues confronting production engineers and service providers on a daily basis.

We have employee built up close working relations with a number of high profile clients ranging from multinational oil companies, UEA, European oil companies, and national Indonesia oil company companies. These relationships have allowed us to receive invaluable feedback, enabling us to make the minute adjustments needed to stay at the forefront of the chemical solutions business.

The formulas are varied, but the most common demulsifiers are:
  • Acid or base catalysed phenol-formaldehyde resins
  • Epoxy resins
  • Polyethyleneimines
  • Polyamines
  • Di-epoxides
  • Polyols
  • Dendrimer
